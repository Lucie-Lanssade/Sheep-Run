Sheepy got lost in the dangerous moutains of Wolfland. He will need your help to defeat wolves and jump over deadly pits. Are you up to the task?

Rules

Sheepy is a lucky sheep, he has 3 lives! Your task is to get him home safe by using your keyboard. First of all, beware of wolves! They take one life out of Sheepy everytime they touch him. But Sheepy is no ordinary sheep: he learned kungg-fu! Get rid of ennemies and udpate his victory score by jumping on them. On his way home, Sheepy also has to overcome perillous pits. Beware of those, because they will take Shippy away forever.
